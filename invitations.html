Invitations

The protocols of entry and participation.

Protocols

How Invitations Work

The Gilded Room is designed as a limited-capacity experience. Invitations may be distributed directly by a host, an event collaborator, or an invited guest who has been given permission to bring someone.

The Entry Sequence:

  1. Receive a digital or printed invitation.
  2. Review the theme, dress code, and arrival window.
  3. Bring the invitation with you.
  4. Present it to the door host.
  5. Whisper the event password when requested.

Possession of a flyer or invitation does not guarantee admission if the invitation has been copied, publicly redistributed, or altered.

Why a Password?

The password is part of the arrival ritual and helps preserve the private, theatrical feeling of each edition. It is not intended to create inconvenience; it simply marks the transition from the ordinary evening outside to the temporary world inside.
