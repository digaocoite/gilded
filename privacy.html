Guest Privacy Policy

The Gilded Room values the privacy of its guests. Event photography, when present, is used selectively. Private collections may be shared through restricted links rather than public social-media galleries.

We believe that private gatherings should remain private. In accordance with this principle, we adhere to the following policies:

  • Restricted Archives: Event photos are stored inside secure systems and are accessible only to verified attendees through private individual links.
  • No Public Distribution: We do not publish images of guests on public social media streams or promotional materials without explicit, prior consent.
  • Opt-Out Options: Guests who prefer not to be photographed can request a specific marker at the entrance to signal the photographer, or contact our team post-event to have any image removed.
  • Data Curation: We do not sell or distribute personal correspondence details, guest names, or list information to third parties.

If you have any questions or would like to request removal of your profile image from a past private collection link, please contact us through our correspondence form.
