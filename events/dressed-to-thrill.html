Dressed to Thrill

Columbia, Missouri

← Back to Traveling Calendar

The Current Invitation

For one evening, The Gilded Room arrives in Columbia with Dressed to Thrill—an after-hours gathering devoted to personal style, dramatic entrances, and the pleasure of being wonderfully overdressed.

This gathering temporarily transforms an unexpected pop-up space in the Arcade District. As a traveling private-event concept, this location is being temporarily utilized and does not represent a permanent Gilded Room venue.

Dress Code Directive

Our theme parameter for Columbia is simple yet demanding: As Fancy as You Dare. We encourage gowns, dinner jackets, avant-garde shapes, cocktail clothing, and any expression of style that pushes beyond the boundaries of standard nightwear. The crowd acts as the room's visual interior; your attire is part of the collective design.

Arrival Ritual

Present your printed or digital invitation at the entrance and whisper the password to the door host. The password for this evening is Champagne.

The password acts as the final transition step, marking your release of the ordinary city streets and entrance into our temporary evening world.

Event Parameters

  • Date: Saturday, July 18, 2026
  • Location: Columbia, MO
  • Setting: The Arcade District Pop-Up
  • Address: 602 Fay St, Columbia, MO 65201
  • Dress Code: As Fancy as You Dare
  • Admission: Invitation Only
  • Password: Champagne
Invitations are nontransferable unless otherwise indicated. The exact entrance instructions may be updated on the day of the event.
